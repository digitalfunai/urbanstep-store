Need to know

Questions,
answered.

Everything you need before your first UrbanStep. Still need help? Talk to our team.

How do UrbanStep sneakers fit?

Most UrbanStep styles fit true to size. For a more secure performance fit, use your usual athletic-shoe size; if you prefer extra room, choose a half size up when available.

How fast will my order arrive?

Orders are prepared within 24 hours. Standard delivery typically arrives in 3–5 business days, while express delivery is available at checkout in eligible locations.

Can I return or exchange a pair?

Yes. Unworn sneakers in their original packaging can be returned or exchanged within 30 days of delivery. Your return label is available from our support team.

How should I care for my sneakers?

Use a soft brush and mild soap with cold water. Air dry away from direct heat, and avoid machine washing to protect the structure and finish of your pair.
